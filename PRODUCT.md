# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Brazilian and international entrepreneurs, mentors, digital specialists, and business owners who are active on Instagram. Typically ages 28-45, already producing content but frustrated by the lack of results. They're busy, don't want to learn dashboards, and communicate primarily via WhatsApp. They care deeply about positioning, authority, and revenue — not vanity metrics. Secondary audience: English-speaking creators and small agencies in international markets (US, Middle East, Europe).

## Product Purpose

Firemode Signal is a competitive intelligence + content editing service delivered entirely via WhatsApp. It spies on competitors' active ads and viral videos, transcribes and reverse-engineers the top content in the client's niche, and returns a ready-to-execute 22-day content war plan plus done-for-you video editing (auto-cut, captions, thumbnails). The goal is to eliminate guesswork from Instagram content strategy. Success: client goes from "what should I post?" to "here's what the market already validated — go record this."

## Brand Personality

Surgical, fast, no-fluff. Like a special ops intelligence briefing — precise, tactical, delivered in seconds. Not a creative agency. Not a SaaS tool. An intelligence operation.

Three words: **Precise. Ruthless. Done.**

Emotional goals: confidence (I know exactly what to post), relief (someone else is doing the thinking), urgency (my competitors are already using this).

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS landing pages with floating mockups and "all-in-one platform" vibes
- Startup-cream aesthetic (white bg, thin sans-serif, turquoise accent, rounded cards)
- Brazilian digital marketing "guru" sites: neon on black, fire emojis everywhere, countdown timers
- Influencer-marketing dashboards that look like analytics software
- Anything with a gradient text hero headline
- Over-animated particle backgrounds or hero video loops with no product value

## Design Principles

1. **Intelligence over decoration** — Every visual element should feel purposeful and data-driven. Nothing decorative for the sake of it.
2. **Friction zero** — The page should communicate instantly, sell the WhatsApp-native simplicity. If it feels complex to browse, the product promise fails.
3. **Confidence through restraint** — Premium positioning means holding back. Not every section needs an icon grid. Silence is expensive.
4. **Dark to light as metaphor** — The existing dark-to-light scroll animation is the brand story: you enter in the dark (no strategy) and emerge into clarity (the plan). Preserve and amplify this.
5. **CTA clarity over CTA volume** — One primary action per section maximum. Reduce decision fatigue.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG 2.1 AA minimum
- High contrast text in both dark and light modes
- Reduced motion support for scroll animations
- Portuguese (PT-BR) primary, English secondary. Both must read naturally, not like machine translation.
